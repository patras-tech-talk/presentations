# May Event [Site Reliability Engineering and Non-tech lessons learned]

**Date:** Tuesday, May 28, 2024, 18:00 - 21:00 EEST
**Venue:** University Of Patras, Department of Economics, Amphitheater-1, Ground floor, Rion Campus

## Talks

### Exploring the World of Site Reliability Engineering
**Speaker:** Eleni Michaletou, Senior Cloud Engineer at Dialectica
**Time:** 18:30 - 19:15

Dive into the fascinating realm of Site Reliability Engineering (SRE). Learn from industry experts as they unravel the core principles, best practices, and real-world applications of SRE. Discover how SRE practices drive reliability, scalability, and efficiency in modern tech ecosystems.

### Beyond the Code: Non-Tech Lessons Learned
**Speaker:** Constantinos Solomonides, SDET Engineer at Juniper Networks
**Time:** 19:15 - 20:00

Stepping beyond the realm of technology to explore invaluable lessons learned in non-tech domains. From the importance of ergonomics in the workplace to navigating the challenges and opportunities of remote work, this session delves into the human-centric aspects of our professional lives.
